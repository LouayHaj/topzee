Scoring Rules Roll the dice. Tap the dice you want to keep, and roll again. After 3 rolls, your turn is over. Tap a category on the scoresheet, and tap the SAVE button. You can score each category only once. After 13 turns, the game is over and your score is saved. Ones
The sum of the dice with one pip
= 3 points
